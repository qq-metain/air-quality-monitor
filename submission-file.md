## Introduction to Application

Air Quality Monitor is a Flutter mobile application designed to help users understand the air quality around their current location and make better daily decisions. The app uses mobile location sensing to detect the user’s position and fetches real-time air quality information, including AQI, PM2.5, PM10, Ozone, Nitrogen Dioxide, Sulfur Dioxide and Carbon Monoxide. The app presents this data through a clear dashboard, pollutant detail cards, historical records and a weekly trend chart.

The app is designed within the Connected Environment theme because it connects the user’s mobile device, physical location, environmental data, external API services and local data storage. It also includes AI-generated health advice to translate raw air quality data into practical suggestions, such as whether it is suitable to go outside, exercise outdoors, or open windows. The app supports repeated interaction by allowing users to review historical records and observe environmental changes over time.
